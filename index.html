Our Meticulous Preparation Process

Proper preparation is essential for a successful stucco painting project. At Klasek Painting, we are dedicated to providing exceptional stucco painting and repair services. Our detailed preparation process ensures that your stucco surface is ready for a flawless paint job

Initial Inspection

We begin with a thorough inspection to identify any cracks, holes, or areas needing repair.

Cleaning

The stucco is power washed to remove dirt, mold, and loose particles, ensuring proper paint adhesion.

Repairing Cracks and Holes

Any imperfections are filled with a high-quality stucco patching compound for a smooth, even surface.

Surface Priming

We apply a specialized primer designed for stucco to improve paint adhesion and provide a uniform finish.

Masking and Covering

Windows, doors, and other areas are carefully protected from paint splatters, ensuring clean lines.

Final Inspection

Before painting, we conduct a final check to ensure all repairs are complete and the surface is perfectly prepared.
